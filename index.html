Why Choose Decision Approach

In today’s dynamic social environment, every individual is compelled to make personal decisions each day in various situations related to career, relationships, and emotions.

However, due to the lack of systematic knowledge about this subject, a person often fails to make the right decisions in such situations. As a result, one has to face various difficulties in life.

Therefore, focusing on the subject of personal decision is the only effective way for solving and preventing most problems.

This subject not only provides immediate solutions to problems but also develops the ability to make long-term right decisions.

Root Cause of Problems

Lack of Structure

Individuals generally do not have any structured concept or process for taking decisions.

Personal Wrong Decisions

Personal Wrong Decisions are the root cause of all Problems — including Personal, Professional or Mental health problems.

Education Gap

The education system has not incorporated any specific principles related to personal decision-making.
